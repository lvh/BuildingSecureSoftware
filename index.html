    (∀ x: T(x) → p(x))

    → (∀ S: (T ⊃ S) → (∀ y: S(y) → p(y)))

    Taught in every intro to (OO) programming

    What don't we teach?

    Least authority

    Don't give stuff more power than it needs.

    Generally not taught except in specialized majors Majors not available at most universities

    POLA > LSP
